About Me

Hello, I'm Sunny Mahil, a highly motivated professional in the analytics field. I hold a bachelor's degree in Paralegal Studies from Humber College and a master's degree in legal studies from Washington University in St. Louis. Currently, I am pursuing an MBA with a focus on Analytics at the University of Missouri – Columbia.

In my role as a Data Validation Analyst at Artech, I have gained invaluable experience in analytics and strategic decision-making. I actively contribute to internal projects aimed at enhancing Artech's operations. I look forward to continuing my career in data analytics, leveraging my expanding skillset, and actively seeking opportunities for personal growth through work and independent projects.

Outside of my educational and professional pursuits, I find joy in reading, gaming, and, above all, traveling. Traveling allows me to disconnect, unwind, and explore new horizons. It's an essential part of my life, providing a much-needed escape and relaxation.
