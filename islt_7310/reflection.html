Reflection

Looking back on my career journey, it's fascinating to see how my path has taken unexpected turns and led me to new and exciting opportunities. From the early days of high school, my fascination with law was evident. The intricacies of the legal field captivated me, and I envisioned myself making a significant impact in this dynamic profession.

However, as I embarked on my educational journey, a gradual shift started to occur. Relocating from Toronto, Canada to the United States presented a unique set of challenges, particularly in terms of my legal aspirations. The lack of American legal experience necessitated a reevaluation of my career trajectory.

Intriguingly, it was during this transition that I found myself naturally gravitating towards the world of corporate sales. The art of negotiation, building relationships, and driving business outcomes resonated with me on a profound level. Surprisingly, my sales career proved to be immensely successful and rewarding, adding an unexpected dimension to my professional profile.

As my journey unfolded, I made another pivotal shift, this time towards the realm of data analytics. Recognizing the increasing importance of data-driven decision-making, I felt drawn to the power of insights and analytics. Embracing this passion, I embarked on the journey of acquiring new skills and knowledge in the field.

In retrospect, these unexpected twists and turns have enriched my career and broadened my horizons. They have taught me the value of adaptability, resilience, and continuous learning. My decision to pursue a master's degree while working full-time exemplifies my commitment to personal and professional growth.

My journey may not have followed a linear path, but it has equipped me with a unique set of skills and perspectives that I am eager to contribute to future endeavors.
